Biography

Hello, I am a Ph.D. candidate in Industrial and Systems Engineering at Virginia Tech, working with Prof. Ran Jin. I simultaneously pursed a Master's degree in Computer Engineering at Virginia Tech under the advice of Prof. Ruoxi Jia. I will join the Industrial and Systems Engineer program housed in the Department of Mechanical and Materials Engineering at the University of Cincinnati in the Fall of 2024.

My research focuses on creating a data quality assurance paradigm to enable effetcive AI modeling perofmance in Manufacturing Industrial Internet. Data generation, data acquisition, data valuation, and data sharing methods have been investigated for AI models with applications in additive manufacturing, bio-manufacturing, semiconducting, and IoT infrastructure design, where statistical and machine learning models as well as optimization methods are heavily used. I also conducted research projects in interpretable machine learning methods, predictive modeling with statistical models, and supply chain optimization by applied operations research.

Two funded Ph.D. positions are available with ideal starting date in Spring 2025. The Ph.D. students are expected to work on Data-centric Artificial Intelligence with real-world application in manufacturing industrial internet and healthcare systems.

Qualified candidates should have:

  • A bachelor’s or master’s degree in Electronic Engineering, Industrial Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, or other related fields.
  • Prior research or project experience in statistical learning, machine learning, programming (particularly in Python), etc.
  • Clear research objectives, strong motivations, and solid math foundations.
  • Good English writing and communication skills.

If you are interested in applying, please send your CV in PDF format to yingyanzeng@vt.edu with subject “ISE Spring 2025 Ph.D.”. Review of applications will begin immediately and continue until the position is filled. Please include a paragraph introducing your research proposal and areas of interest. Selected applicants will be asked to present an assigned paper. Please feel free to reach out to me for additional information or any questions regarding the positions.
